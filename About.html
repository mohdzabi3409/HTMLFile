Our story, mission, and values

Our Story

Founded in 2020, ShopFolio began as a small startup with a big vision: to create a marketplace that showcases the best products for portfolios across all industries.

Our founders, Jane Doe and John Smith, recognized the need for a centralized platform where professionals could find high-quality products to enhance their portfolios.

Today, we've grown into a leading e-commerce platform serving thousands of customers worldwide with a curated selection of premium products.
